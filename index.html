5 Countries. 5 Days.
1 Unforgettable Journey.

Our most recent cohort of 205 explorers from 7 nations traveled across continents — virtually — experiencing culture, history, food, and friendship.

🏔️ 🇳🇬 Day 1

Jos, Nigeria

Explored the Plateau State highlands — tin mining history, diverse ethnic cuisine, and conversations with local young artisans.

⚽ 🇬🇧 Day 2

Liverpool, UK

Discovered the Beatles' birthplace, maritime heritage, and modern multiculturalism through the eyes of young Liverpudlians.

🏰 🇬🇭 Day 3

Cape Coast, Ghana

Walked through history at Cape Coast Castle, explored Kakum National Park canopy walkway, and tasted traditional Fante dishes.

🏛️ 🇵🇱 Day 4

Warsaw, Poland

Explored the rebuilt Old Town, learned about Polish resilience, and connected with young Poles over pierogi and technology.

🦁 🇰🇪 Day 5

Narok County, Kenya

Joined a Maasai cultural immersion — wildlife, traditional dance, bead-making, and stories around a virtual campfire.

Powered by Blockchain
Learning Credentials
That Last Forever

Every achievement is recorded on the Polygon blockchain — creating permanent, verifiable, child-owned proof of learning that no institution can revoke.

🎓

Soulbound Credentials

When your child completes a tour, they receive a non-transferable NFT credential on Polygon. It's permanently tied to them — verifiable by universities, employers, and scholarship committees worldwide.

⬡ Verified on Polygon
💰

Transparent Scholarships

5% of every registration fee goes to the Explorer Scholarship Fund — a smart contract that awards tours to underserved children. Every donation is publicly traceable from donor to child impact.

🔍 Fully Auditable On-Chain
⚡

Instant School Payments

Partner schools receive their commissions instantly via smart contract — no manual tracking, no month-end reconciliation, no payment disputes. Every split is recorded on the public ledger.

🤝 Zero Commission Delays
🛡️

Child-Owned Data

Your child's learning records belong to them — not to GET, not to their school, not to any government. Even if GET ceases to exist, their credentials live on the blockchain permanently.
