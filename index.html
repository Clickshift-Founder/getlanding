More Than a Tour
We Don't Just Show Them the World. We Change Theirs.

Every tour is engineered to develop skills, shift perspectives, and build the foundations of global citizenship — not passive screen time.

🌐

Global Perspective

Children interact with peers and guides in different countries, breaking the bubble of their immediate environment. They learn the world is vast, diverse, and full of possibility.

Learning Credentials That Last Forever

Every achievement is recorded on Polygon — creating permanent, verifiable, child-owned proof of learning.

🎓

Soulbound Credentials

When your child completes a tour, they receive a non-transferable NFT on Polygon — permanently tied to them, verifiable by universities, employers, and scholarship committees.

⬡ Verified on Polygon
💰

Transparent Scholarships

5% of every registration goes to the Explorer Scholarship Fund — a smart contract that awards tours to underserved children. Every donation is publicly traceable.

🔍 Fully Auditable On-Chain
⚡

Instant School Payments

Partner schools receive commissions instantly via smart contract — no manual tracking, no month-end reconciliation, no payment disputes.

🤝 Zero Commission Delays
🛡️

Child-Owned Data

Your child's records belong to them — not to GET, not to any school. Even if GET ceases to exist, their credentials live on the blockchain permanently.
